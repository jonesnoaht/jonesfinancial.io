Welcome to our page for purchasing NFTs from drops. This page is secured via its hash address through IPFS, and the redirect to this page is secured at the level of the domain-name server (DNS). To quickly check this page, bookmark http://nft.jonesfinancial.io and tap the bookmark to refresh the page. Once the DNS has been updated, the URL http://nft.jonesfinancial.io will direct you to a different page on IPFS where you will be able to access the NFT drop. Hang tight and refresh your page once we have hit the drop time. If you see this page again, refresh again.
