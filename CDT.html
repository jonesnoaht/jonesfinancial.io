CDT Summary

  1. Credit Unions are non-profit entities with the stated mission of promoting the economic prosperity of local communities.

  2. Certificates of deposits, just like any other account, are insured up to 250,000 USD by the FDIC for banks or the NCUA for a credit union.

  3. The Federal Reserve Bank provides inexpensive audits that can be put on-chain.

  4. Thus, CDs that are federally insured are among the most secure ways to convert fiat currency to a cryptographic stabletoken.

Efficiency

The CDT benefits local communities because the Cardano network has lower transaction fees and lower faster settlement times when compared to the Visa-Mastercard network.

Banks that issue these loans will benefit from increasing their customer bases, have increased transparency of the movement of funds when making loans, and will be able to offer financial services via smart contracts that would otherwise not be feasible.

Security

Because each CDT issuance is discrete, each issuance will be generated by different credit unions and banks. Given NCUA and FDIC insurance and a standardized mechanism for auditability via the Federal Reserve Bank Services, any bank or credit union will be able to issue CDs as CDT, and the scope and impact of fraudulent issuances, should they even be possible, are automatically limited in scope.

We will be offering scrambled seed phrases engraved in steel and stored in safety deposit boxes. One copy will be be given to the customer, and the other copy will be stored in a safety deposit box.

In our first issuances, KYC will have already been handled as the service will at first only be made available to those who already have an account with the credit union.

Business Model

Fees for CDT will be nominal to cover costs. Jones Financial will benefit financially as we will provide training and support services to businesses and financial institutions seeking to integrate CDT into their financial systems.
